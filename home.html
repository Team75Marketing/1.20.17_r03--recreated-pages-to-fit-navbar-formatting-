OUR MISSION



Team 75 strives to promote STEM, FIRST, and education.
We strive to uphold the values set forth by our sponsors and supporters. We strive to uphold the values set forth by
Johnson & Johnson’s credo as well as Team 75’s, and use them as guides to give back to our community.
We promise to extend the message of FIRST, and support all of its programs. We will empower rookie and young
teams in all FIRST programs to aid their success and growth.
We strive to make a difference in the world using what we know, and what we can learn.
This is the mission Team 75 has set forth to guide our advancement.
