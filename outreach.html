Outreach

TIW

Technology Innovation Week

Technology Innovation Week, TIW, is a law, N.J.S.A. 36:2-147, our team worked with NJ State Legislature to pass in 2010. It is recognized during the first week of March. It encourages teachers across New Jersey schools to incorporate Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) into their curriculum TIW spreads the recognition and inspiration of science and technology to students.

WISE

Women in Science and Engineering

Created by Team 75 in 2006, WISE, short for Women in Science and Engineering, is an initiative aimed at interesting young women in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematical) fields. Historically, science and engineering careers have employed primarily men. WISE exists to reach out to young women who may not otherwise be interested in science and technology.

FIRST Involvement

FLL and BoroBlast

Team 75 was successful in bringing the FIRST Lego League (FLL) to the Hillsborough School District in 2006 with three teams in the Intermediate and Middle Schools. Team 75 also hosts an annual FLL regional competition called BoroBlast every November.

Bike Build

The purpose of the Bike Build is to give back to the community through a unique event that allows teams participating at a FIRST robotics Regional to work together in the midst of a competition, consequently encouraging team bonding and community services.
